About Me


Childhood


I was born in 1991 in a small town in Hungary, where I finished the Primary and High school educations.


University years


I started the University in 2010 at BUTE as a MECHATRONICAL ENGINEER and graduated in 2014.


After the Bsc I started here the MECHANICAL ENGINEERING MODELLING Msc which was in English.


The topic of my thesis were Strength analysis of wiper system parts at Bsc and Theoretical analysis of damages in case of rail transportation at Msc.


I have written a dissertation for a student conference (TDK) with one of my friends about a vibrational phenomena. I was also a demonstrator at Applied Mechanics department in 2012.


Professional years


I started at BOSCH in 2013 as a SIMULATION ENGINEER TRAINEE and I became a full time employee in 2016 as a SYSTEM ENGINEER. I have been working here since than.


Mainly I deal with ABAQUS SCRIPTING IN PYTHON, but I also performed some WEB DEVELOPMENT TASKS (symfony,vue.js etc.) during the years.
